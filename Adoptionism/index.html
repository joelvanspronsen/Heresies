Adoptionism
(later known as Arianism)

Description

(later known as Arianism) - The belief that God adopted Christ as his son by Jesus earning it Denies Christ’s virgin birth

Debates

It was rejected by the Synods of Antioch and the First Council of Nicaea

Official responses to these Heresies

Nicene creed- we believe in one Lord, Jesus Christ,… eternally begotten of the Father,, Apostles creed- “His Only begotten son, Our Lord” (Jesus was)...“conceived by the Holy Spirit”
